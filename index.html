Yinong He

Hi there! I am a senior undergraduate student at University of Michigan majoring in Data Science Engineering. I am also dual majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research focuses on human-centered artificial intelligence and robotics. I am interested in enhancing reinforcement learning with human language feedback, motion planning in robotic manipulation, reasoning within complex dynamics, such as deformable objects. I am fortunate to work under the guidance of Prof. Joyce Chai and Prof. Dmitry Berenson.
